Alika's Treehouse

An interactive “pop-up book” for children of the digital age, Alika’s Treehouse tells the story of a young city girl, Alika, as she spends the summer building a treehouse with her grandfather.

About Alika Media

Using Grandpop Nevin simple analogies and storytelling, readers will work alongside Alika with hands-on exercises that teach basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript skills while incrementally building their own digital treehouse.

This product not only engages readers, it allows them to empathize with the heroine as she conquers the frustrations and experiences the excitement of learning to code
